Sjalavard (Swedish for "soul care") is an AI-powered Christian life coaching app that walks with you through the thoughts that weigh you down — helping you catch the lies your mind tells you, replace them with what is actually true, and find your way back to freedom.

WHAT SJALAVARD DOES

When you open a session, you talk to Sjal — your personal spiritual coach. Sjal listens, asks the right questions, and guides you through a structured arc grounded in three proven frameworks:

• CBT (David Burns) — identify the thinking trap fueling your feelings
• Neuroscience (Dr. Caroline Leaf) — understand how thoughts shape your brain
• Christian wisdom — voices like Brennan Manning, Dallas Willard, and Richard Foster woven in naturally

Every session closes with a personalized summary, one concrete action step, and a moment of gratitude.

FEATURES

✦ AI Soul Care Sessions — 4–6 exchange coaching conversations with Sjal, tailored to your profile and focused goal. No two sessions are the same.

🙏 Prayer Journal — A private, AI-free space to write and keep your prayers. Mark them answered over time. Never processed by AI.

💙 Daily Check-In — A 10-second mood pulse at the top of every day. One word, one optional note. Builds a picture of your emotional health over time.

📖 Daily Verse — A rotating scripture verse greets you each day.

📊 Mood Trend Chart — See your last 14 days of check-ins and session moods visualized at a glance.

🔍 Cognitive Distortion Identifier — Tap to name the thinking trap fueling your feelings, drawn from David Burns' framework in Feeling Good.

⚡ Action Steps — Every session ends with one spiritually grounded action step to carry into your week.

🗂 Session History — All completed sessions saved and accessible on any device.

🚨 Crisis Support — If your words suggest you may be in crisis, Sjalavard automatically surfaces the 988 Lifeline before continuing.

⚙️ Personalized Profile — Set up once. Your name, what you're working through, your focused goal, and your favorite Christian authors shape how Sjal coaches you.

🔒 Cross-Device Sync — Your sessions, prayers, and check-ins sync securely across all your devices.

PRIVACY

No ads. No tracking. No data selling. Your prayer journal is never processed by AI. Session content is used only to power your coaching responses. Full privacy policy is in the Legal tab.

Sjalavard is not a replacement for therapy. If you are in a mental health crisis, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988) or seek care from a qualified professional.
